About

January G. Msemakweli is a Tanzanian Environmental Health Practitioner, Researcher, Academician, and Data Analyst. An alumnus of the Muhimbili University of Health and Allied Sciences (MUHAS), he is passionate about epidemiology, data science, and public health.

In 2024, January co-authored a review article titled "One Health Approach to Antimicrobial Resistance: Integrating Human, Animal, and Environmental Perspectives," published in the Journal of Public Health and Community Medicine.

Beyond his research, January is an Information and Technology Enthusiast and Content Creator. He shares insights on environmental health and data analysis through various platforms, including his Instagram account.

January is also active on GitHub, where he showcases his skills in data analysis and epidemiology, particularly using R.

Through his multifaceted expertise and dedication, January continues to contribute significantly to the fields of environmental health and public health in Tanzania and beyond.
